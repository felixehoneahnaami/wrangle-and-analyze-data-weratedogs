1. Correctness of Predictions¶

First, let's take a look at the confidence levels of the predictions made for the dog images on the account. From the histogram below, we can see that most of the data for both the second and third predictions are skewed to the right while that of the first prediction is skewed to the left.

This shows a very high level of confidence in the first prediction (p1_conf) compared to the second (p2_conf) and third predictions (p3_conf). This means that the neural network used to make the predictions is highly certain when it comes to the first prediction.

4. Analysis of Retweet and Favorite Count¶

Finally, we can also see the relationship between the number of retweets and favorites on a tweet. Suggestions for WeRateDogs Twitter account¶

We can also use this data analysis to make suggestions for the WeRateDogs Twitter account. For example, since the majority of the dogs featured are of the "pupper" breed, the account could focus on featuring more unique and diverse breeds to appeal to a wider audience. Additionally, since there is a strong correlation between retweets and favorites, the account could also focus on strategies to increase engagement and viral spread on their tweets.
